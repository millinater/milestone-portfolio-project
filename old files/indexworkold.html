Outside of designing and coding this cool website, here are some other examples of my work.

During my classes with Skillcrush, we were tasked to design a logo and business card for our web design and development company. I created the logo using Adobe InDesign and the business card using Adobe Illustrator.

In my role as a Group Sales Manager for the Renaissance Montgomery Hotel & Spa at the Convention Center, I was tasked to make several marketing and promotion publications. This particular project was an email footer to inform clients of the Group Promotion for an Apple Watch. I created this image and used Canva to design the layout, font pairings, and flow, as well as the written copy.

These two examples are for seperate group promotions that I designed and created using Microsoft Office Publisher. These PDFs are interactive and contain links to several areas of the hotel's website for meeting information and contact forms to submit a Request for Proposal. The images were altered using Adobe Photoshop and Microsoft Publisher.

The Renaissance Montgomery Hotel & Spa at the Convention Center document to the left, was created for a tradeshow event. The idea was to stand out from hundreds of other hotels and venues. The magazine table of contents layout was a creative way to showcase important hotel facts to meeting planners. This document was designed and created using Microsoft Publisher.
The document on the right is a presentation that I created for a mock group sales presentation. We were given information about our madeup company and their meeting needs. The goal was to give a creative, personalized, and influential sales presentation. The company was in broadcasting, so I created a presentation based off of a news broadcast format- to include topic handoffs, graphics, and breaking news.
